Teaching

I teach at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics. Courses include:

Public Budgeting and Finance

This course introduces fundamental concepts and practices in public budgeting and financial management. Topics include budget preparation, revenue and expenditure analysis, financial management practices, and public sector accounting principles.

Study on Tax Theory and Practice

Comprehensive study of tax theory and its practical applications in modern economies.

Methods and Applications of Policy Analysis

Advanced techniques and tools for analyzing public policies and their implementation.
